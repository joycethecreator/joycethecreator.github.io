## Overview

This is a one-person project created for a French Cinema course under a strict 48-hour production constraint. The objective was to complete a fully realized narrative film while emulating the style and tone of a selected French movie, with emphasis on pacing, framing, and restrained storytelling.

---

## Roles
**Writer 路 Director 路 Cinematographer 路 Actor 路 Editor**

- Wrote the script and developed the concept
- Directed and shot the film under a strict time constraint
- Performed on camera
- Edited the final cut using **Adobe Premiere Pro**
- Added lightweight visual effects using **Adobe After Effects**

---

## Process
The compressed timeline required:
- rapid ideation and script development
- minimal-location shooting with deliberate framing
- streamlined post-production workflows
