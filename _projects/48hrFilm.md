## Overview
This project was created as part of a **French Cinema course**, with the challenge of completing a full short film within **48 hours**. The creative goal was to **mimic the style and tone of French cinema**, focusing on pacing, framing, and narrative simplicity.

---

## Roles
**Writer 路 Director 路 Cinematographer 路 Actor 路 Editor**

- Wrote the script and developed the concept
- Directed and shot the film under a strict time constraint
- Performed on camera
- Edited the final cut using **Adobe Premiere Pro**
- Added simple visual effects using **Adobe After Effects**

---

## Process
The compressed timeline required:
- rapid ideation and script development
- efficient location planning and shooting
- streamlined post-production workflows

Creative decisions prioritized mood and storytelling over technical complexity, aligning with stylistic elements commonly found in French film.

---

## Outcome
The project demonstrates the ability to:
- manage an end-to-end film production independently
- make clear creative decisions under pressure
- balance storytelling, cinematography, and post-production within a fixed deadline
